IT IS WHAT IT IS BRUH

Ever since i could remember, i have allowed a certain level of bullshit into my life. You know, not too much to where im completely lost but just enough to fuck some shit up.

But! no matter how hard or atrocious my story is, it isnt an excuse to do nothing! And nothing comes for free. Did i really expect to just have things that i didnt work for and thus didnt deserve fall into my lap?! no!
it doesnt work that way... and shame on anyone for thinking otherwise.
i grew up in Los Angeles, California. most of my friends are either dead or in jail.. i know how cliche' that sounds but whats even more cliche' is how common that story is.

I remember one Thanksgiving, all the kids were playing in the front yard, when a car drove by and started shooting at my family's house. My little cousin Vick was struck in the head and killed. ill never understand why things happend the way they do. why someone so young was not allowed to experience life and all it has to offer.

...AND IT IS WHAT IT'S GON BE...

Here I am!... this is me!... no one can stop me but me! to keep thinking negative is to continue to bring about negative. one must be prepared to think different!.. change their thoughts... thats the only way to bring about change.

self love is important. self assurance is important... read, learn, pick up new skills!..eat clean, workout. create a program that works for you. your success cant be predicated on someone elses success. at some point, we have to stand on our own two. i dont say that to validate the efforts of those less sensitive to the plight of people who have less. i say this to say; if you sit in shit long enough, you stop smelling it... it doesnt stop stinking though... at the end of the day, its up to you.
